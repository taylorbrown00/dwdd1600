Special Events

Formal Dances
We make corsages and boutonnieres for events like high school dances or even weddings.
Weddings
Weddings involve lots of different flower arrangements but the first most important is the boquete for the bride. Like we also stated sometime corsages and boutonnieres are used as well. We also have done flower crowns or necklaces.

Location Set Up

Backdrop
A big hit is always our flower backdrop, ranging anywhere from arches to fully flower filled panels. It is always great for the photo opportunities at events.
Staircase
At most locations there is a staircase, and it is always begging to be decorated on the handrails!
Tables
The tables also deserve to look good. We can do small vase arrangements, or even full table length flower paths.

Funeral

Photo Frame
In honor of loved ones who have passed we love to frame pictures of them in flower arrangements.
Casket Top
Another way to incorperate the flowers is to use and arrangement on the top of the casket.

Preserved

Framed
All of our flower arrangements can be preserved into frames of all shapes and sizes.
Jewlery
